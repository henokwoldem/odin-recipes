Cereal Recipe:

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl
  • Your favourite cereal
  • Cold milk(any kind)

Instructions

  1. Put cereal in the bowl.
  2. Pour milk over the cereal (stop before it overflows).

Optional Upgrades

  • Add sliced bananas or strawberries
  • Add a spoon of peanut butter
  • Add cinnamon
  • Use chocolate milk for dessert-level cereal
