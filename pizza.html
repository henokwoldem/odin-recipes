Pizza Recipe:

Ingredients:

For the dough (optional but easy):

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp instant yeast

OR:
Use store-bought pizza dough or naan/tortillas for a very quick version.

For the toppings:

  • ½ cup pizza sauce (jarred or marinara)
  • 1–2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Any toppings you like: pepperoni, vegetables, cooked chicken, mushrooms, etc.

Steps:

1. Make the Dough (10 minutes + rest)

  1. Mix warm water + sugar + yeast → wait 5 minutes for bubbles.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil.
  3. Mix and knead for 3–5 minutes until smooth.
  4. Let rise for 30–45 minutes (or skip rise for extra quick pizza; it still works).

2. Shape the Pizza

  • Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C)
  • Roll out the dough into a circle or rectangle
  • Place onto baking sheet or pizza pan

3. Add Toppings

  1. Spread pizza sauce
  2. Sprinkle mozzarella
  3. Add any toppings you like

4. Bake

  • Bake 12–15 minutes
  • Done when crust is golden and cheese is melted and slightly browned
