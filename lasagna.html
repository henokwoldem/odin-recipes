LASAGNA RECIPE

A bolognese based lasagna

The following recipe is a ground beef based lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce or 3 cups homemade tomato sauce
  • 1-2 tbsp tomato paste (optional, for richnes)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • ½ tsp red chili flakes (optional)
For the cheese mixture:
  • 1 ½ cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 tsp dried parsley or basil
  • Pinch of salt + pepper
Other:
  • 9–12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 2–3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil for cooking

Steps:

1. Make the Meat Sauce

  1. Heat a pan with a little olive oil.
  2. Add onion → cook until soft.
  3. Add garlic → cook 30 seconds.
  4. Add ground beef → cook until browned.
  5. Add seasoning (salt, pepper, Italian seasoning)
  6. Stir in marinara sauce + tomato paste.
  7. Simmer for 15–20 minutes so it thickens.

2. Mix the Cheese Filling

In a bowl, combine:

  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan
  • Egg
  • Parsley/basil
  • Salt + pepper

Mix until smooth.

3. Boil Noodles (if not oven-ready)

  • Cook in salted water until al dente
  • Drain and lay flat to prevent sticking

4. Assemble the Lasagna

In a 9x13 baking dish:
  1. Thin layer of meat sauce (prevents sticking)
  2. Layer of noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture (spread evenly)
  4. Meat sauce
  5. Mozzarella

5. Bake

  • Cover with foil (so cheese doesn’t burn)
  • Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes
  • Remove foil and bake another 20 minutes until bubbly and golden

6. Rest and Serve

Let the lasagna rest 10–15 minutes before cutting.
his helps it set so slices come out perfectly.
